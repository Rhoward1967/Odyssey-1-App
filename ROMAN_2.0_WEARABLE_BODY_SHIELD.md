## 1. The Physics Framework: "The Braking Cascade"

### 1.1 Fundamental Equation

The system solves the **Kinetic Energy equation**:

$$KE = \frac{1}{2}mv^2$$

Where:
- $m$ = mass of projectile
- $v$ = velocity of projectile

**Critical Insight:** Because velocity ($v$) is squared, even a slight reduction in speed creates a massive drop in impact force.

### 1.2 Three-Phase Energy Management

#### **Phase 1: Harmonic Deceleration**
The suit emits a high-frequency pulse that creates a "pressure wall" in the air surrounding the wearer. This field disrupts the projectile's flight path, reducing its effective velocity before physical contact.

**Mechanism:** Silver-nylon conductive threads act as a wearable antenna array, generating a focused acoustic pressure field at 20-40 kHz.

#### **Phase 2: Molecular Locking**
The Shear Thickening Fluid (STF) embedded within the weave transitions from liquid to solid state upon high-velocity impact, creating an instantaneous rigid barrier.

**Mechanism:** Non-Newtonian fluid response—particles lock together under shear stress, creating a temporary armor plate at the exact impact location.

#### **Phase 3: Work-Energy Conversion**
The physical "push" of the object compressing the suit layers is converted into electrical current through piezoelectric fiber compression.

**Mechanism:** Piezo-fiber lattice deformation generates DC voltage proportional to impact force, which is then regulated and stored in the suit's battery system.
