## THE COUNTER-CANON: AMENDMENT RECORD PROTOCOL

The **Counter-Canon: A Dictionary of Living Law** (Volumes 1–9) published in this book is the **First Edition** of a living legal reference. Every entry contains two zones:

**THE ETERNAL ZONE** — The Embedded Assumption and The Truth. These sections never change. History does not change. Truth does not change.

**THE LIVING ZONE** — The Dominant Definition, Counter-Definition, Authority, and Practical Application. These sections are updated as case law develops, statutes change, and international instruments are issued or amended.

### Update Protocol

Every significant legal development — Supreme Court decision, major federal statute, circuit court ruling, or binding international instrument — that materially affects any Counter-Canon entry triggers an amendment. The amendment process:

1. **Monitor** — The R.O.M.A.N. 2.0 AI engine continuously tracks new legal developments across all four source categories.
2. **Analyze** — The Claude API evaluates each development against every affected entry and prepares a proposed amendment with full citations.
3. **Review** — Every proposed amendment is reviewed and approved by Rickey Allan Howard before publication. The AI analyzes. The author decides.
4. **Publish** — Approved amendments are integrated into the entry. The Amendment Record is updated. A new edition of the affected volume is released.
5. **Archive** — Every prior edition is permanently preserved. The First Edition you hold is a fixed, timestamped document that will remain in the public archive indefinitely.

### Edition Naming

Each updated volume is identified by: **Volume Number · Edition Number · Year**
Example: *Counter-Canon, Vol. I, Ed. 3, 2029*
