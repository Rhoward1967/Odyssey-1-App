### 1.1 What OSC Is

The O-1 Sovereignty Credit (OSC) is a **closed-loop utility credit** issued by
Odyssey-1 AI LLC. It represents a pre-paid unit of access to the Odyssey-1 AI
system and the IP portfolio of the Howard Jones Bloodline Ancestral Trust.

OSC is:
- A Digital Tool — its value derives from the functional operation of the
  Odyssey-1 system, not from managerial efforts or passive yield
- A Pre-Paid Service Credit — equivalent to a gift card, arcade token, or
  airline mile; purchased to be consumed, not to be invested

### 2.3 Deflationary Burn Mechanism

Every OSC transaction triggers a **0.5% burn**:
- 0.5% of the OSC cost of each service call is permanently destroyed
- Burned OSC is removed from the total supply permanently
- As usage increases, total supply decreases → remaining OSC becomes more scarce
- No entity — including Odyssey-1 AI LLC — can un-burn destroyed credits

**Effect:** The more the system is used, the more valuable each remaining OSC becomes.

### 4.2 Digital Currency Acquisition (Oracle Bridge)

Users may also acquire OSC using accepted digital currencies:
- Bitcoin (BTC)
- Ethereum (ETH)
- Other approved assets (Managing Officer discretion)

**Oracle Price-Lock Process:**
1. User selects "Buy OSC with BTC/ETH"
2. Oracle fetches real-time market price
3. System issues a **60-second price lock**
4. User sends digital currency to a one-time-use receiving address
5. System confirms receipt on-chain
6. OSC issued immediately to user's internal account
7. Received digital currency moves to the multi-sig treasury vault

**Legal Classification:** This is a barter exchange — the user trades a digital
commodity for a service utility credit. Not a securities trade.

### 4.3 No Cash-Out

**OSC cannot be redeemed for fiat or digital currency.**

OSC can only be:
- Spent on Odyssey-1 services
- Transferred between Odyssey-1 accounts (at Managing Officer's discretion)

This closed-loop architecture is the primary legal protection of this system.
Any modification to allow fiat redemption requires attorney review before implementation.
