## I. THE TWO ZONES: WHAT CHANGES AND WHAT DOES NOT

Every entry in the Counter-Canon is divided into two permanent zones. This division is the architectural core of the entire project. It is what makes the Counter-Canon simultaneously current and eternal.

### The Living Zone

The Living Zone contains the elements of each entry that are subject to legal evolution. As case law develops, statutes change, agency interpretations shift, and international instruments are ratified or amended, the Living Zone is updated to reflect the current state of the law. The Living Zone contains:

- **The Dominant Definition:** updated when authoritative sources revise the word's primary legal meaning.
- **The Counter-Definition:** expanded, refined, or strengthened when new cases or statutes provide additional legal grounding.
- **The Authority:** updated with new citations when new decisions, statutes, or instruments support or challenge the counter-definition.
- **The Practical Application:** updated when procedural or substantive changes affect how the counter-definition is deployed.

Every change to the Living Zone is logged in the Amendment Record with the date, the triggering authority, and the nature of the change. No change is made without a documented legal basis.

### The Eternal Zone

The Eternal Zone contains the elements of each entry that are not subject to legal evolution — because they are not legal arguments. They are truths. The Eternal Zone contains:

- **The Embedded Assumption:** a historical and analytical fact about why the dominant definition was written as it was. History does not change.
- **The Truth:** the irreducible statement about the living being's nature that no court, no statute, and no definition can ultimately contradict. Truth does not change.

**KEY POINT:** The Eternal Zone is what makes the Counter-Canon something more than a legal reference. Every other legal dictionary is entirely in the Living Zone — it tracks what the law says. The Counter-Canon also tracks what is true regardless of what the law says. That distinction is its entire purpose.

## II. THE EDITION MODEL: HOW VOLUMES AND UPDATES WORK

### Volumes

The Counter-Canon is organized into volumes by alphabetical range. Volume One covers C, L, P, and S — the foundational words of the doctrine. Subsequent volumes expand the coverage alphabetically while deepening the treatment of words already entered. Each volume is a complete, standalone publication with its own ISBN, its own publication date, and its own amendment record.

### Editions

Each volume is published in dated editions. The First Edition is the founding text. Subsequent editions are triggered by one of three conditions:

1. **Significant Legal Change:** A Supreme Court decision, major federal statute, or binding international instrument materially changes the legal landscape for one or more entries. This triggers an expedited edition.
2. **Scheduled Annual Review:** Every calendar year, every entry in every published volume is reviewed against the current state of the law. Any entry requiring update triggers a new edition of its volume.
3. **New Word Coverage:** When a new set of words is sufficiently developed to meet the entry format standard, a new volume is published.

### Edition Naming Convention

Each edition is identified by Volume Number, Edition Number, and Publication Year. Example: Counter-Canon, Vol. I, Ed. 3, 2029. This naming convention allows precise citation in legal proceedings: a court brief can cite Counter-Canon Vol. I, Ed. 1, 2026, page 12, and every reader can locate the exact text being cited. Earlier editions are preserved permanently in the public archive.

**KEY POINT:** The edition model makes the Counter-Canon legally citable in a way that a website never can be. A webpage changes without record. A published edition is a fixed, timestamped document that exists permanently. The law respects permanence.

## III. THE AMENDMENT RECORD: THE LEGAL SIGNIFICANCE OF TRACKING CHANGE

The Amendment Record is one of the Counter-Canon's most powerful features. Every change to every Living Zone entry is documented in the Amendment Record with four elements:

- **Date:** The date the amendment was made.
- **Triggering Authority:** The specific case, statute, or instrument that necessitated the change. Cited in full.
- **Nature of Change:** What changed: a new citation added, a counter-definition expanded, an authority superseded, a practical application updated.
- **Editorial Note:** A brief explanation of why the change matters for the entry's argument.

### Why the Amendment Record Is a Legal Argument

The Amendment Record does more than document change. It makes an argument. When the Amendment Record shows that the Counter-Canon's counter-definition of Person was strengthened by three new Supreme Court decisions over five years, that trend is itself evidence that the law is moving in the direction the Counter-Canon identified. When the Amendment Record shows that a dominant definition has been narrowed by Congress, that narrowing supports the argument that the embedded assumption was always contestable.

The Amendment Record turns the Counter-Canon into a longitudinal legal argument — not just a snapshot of what the law says today, but a documented record of where the law has been, where it is, and where the pressure of truth is pushing it.

### Sample Amendment Record Entry Format

```
AMENDMENT RECORD — VOLUME ONE
Word: PERSON
Edition: 2 | Date: [DATE OF UPDATE]
Triggering Authority: [Case Name], [Citation] ([Year])
Nature of Change: Counter-Definition expanded. New authority
  added to Authority section. Practical Application updated
  to reflect new procedural context created by decision.
Editorial Note: The Court's holding in [Case] strengthens
  the Counter-Canon's position that natural personhood
  precedes and is not derived from legal personhood.
  Eternal Zone: unchanged.
```

## IV. THE CLAUDE API UPDATE ENGINE: HOW IT WORKS

The update engine is powered by the Anthropic Claude API. It operates in three stages: monitoring, analysis, and flagging. It does not make editorial decisions. It identifies what needs human review and prepares the analysis for that review.

### Stage One: Monitoring

The monitoring layer tracks new legal developments across four source categories:

- **Supreme Court Decisions:** Every opinion issued by the U.S. Supreme Court is processed. The engine identifies which Counter-Canon headwords appear in the opinion or are addressed by its holding.
- **Federal Circuit Court Decisions:** Published opinions from all thirteen federal circuit courts are monitored for decisions that significantly interpret headwords or affect the counter-definitions' authority citations.
- **Major Federal Legislation:** New federal statutes that define, redefine, or materially affect headwords trigger an entry review.
- **International Instruments:** New UN declarations, ICCPR committee findings, IACHR decisions, and ILO conventions that affect headwords are monitored through public international law databases.

### Stage Two: Analysis

When a new legal development is identified as potentially relevant to a headword, the Claude API performs the following analysis:

1. Read the full text of the new decision, statute, or instrument.
2. Identify every passage that bears on the headword's meaning, scope, or application.
3. Evaluate whether the development strengthens, weakens, expands, or narrows the Counter-Canon's counter-definition.
4. Identify whether any existing authority citation has been superseded, overruled, or limited by the new development.
5. Draft a proposed amendment to the Living Zone reflecting the analysis.
6. Draft the Amendment Record entry for the proposed change.
7. Flag the entry for editorial review with a priority level: Urgent (overruling authority), Standard (significant development), or Informational (relevant but not requiring update).

### Stage Three: Flagging

Every flagged entry is delivered to the editorial review queue with: the full analysis, the proposed amendment, the draft Amendment Record entry, and the source documents. The editor — Rickey Allan Howard — reviews every flagged item before any change is published. The AI analyzes. The author decides.

### The Claude API Prompt Architecture

The update engine uses a structured prompt that encodes the Counter-Canon's methodology directly. The core prompt instructs Claude to analyze new legal developments against existing entries using the following framework:

```
COUNTER-CANON UPDATE ANALYSIS PROMPT (CORE STRUCTURE):

You are the update engine for the Counter-Canon: A Dictionary
of Living Law. Your role is to analyze new legal developments
against existing entries and prepare proposed amendments.

For each new development you are given:
  1. Read the full text carefully.
  2. Identify every passage bearing on any Counter-Canon headword.
  3. For each relevant headword, evaluate:
     a. Does this development strengthen the counter-definition?
     b. Does it weaken or limit it?
     c. Does it supersede any existing authority citation?
     d. Does it require updating the Practical Application?
  4. Apply the Two Zone Rule:
     - The Eternal Zone (Embedded Assumption, The Truth) is
       NEVER updated. Never propose changes to these zones.
     - Only the Living Zone may be amended.
  5. Draft a proposed amendment stating exactly what changes
     and exactly why, with full citations.
  6. Draft the Amendment Record entry.
  7. Assign priority: URGENT / STANDARD / INFORMATIONAL.

The Counter-Canon's foundational principle: the law's own
language contains the truth the dominant definitions suppress.
Your analysis must always look for what the new development
reveals about that truth — not merely what it says about the
dominant definition.
```

**KEY POINT:** The prompt architecture encodes the Counter-Canon's methodology into the AI itself. The update engine does not just track what changed. It evaluates what changed means for the living being's rights. That distinction is what makes it the Counter-Canon's engine rather than a generic legal monitoring tool.

## V. THE UPDATE WORKFLOW: FROM NEW CASE LAW TO PUBLISHED ENTRY

The complete workflow from triggering event to published amendment:

**Step 1. New Legal Development Identified**
A new Supreme Court decision, federal statute, or international instrument is published. The monitoring layer identifies it as potentially relevant to one or more Counter-Canon headwords.

**Step 2. Full Text Retrieval**
The complete text of the new development is retrieved from the source. For cases: the full opinion including concurrences and dissents. For statutes: the full enrolled text. For international instruments: the full adopted text.

**Step 3. Claude API Analysis**
The update engine runs the full analysis described in Section IV. It produces: a relevance determination, an impact assessment for each affected entry, proposed amendments to the Living Zone, draft Amendment Record entries, and a priority flag.

**Step 4. Editorial Review Queue**
All flagged items are delivered to the editorial review queue with full supporting documentation. Urgent items are flagged for immediate review. Standard items are reviewed on a weekly cycle. Informational items are reviewed monthly.

**Step 5. Editorial Decision**
The author reviews the analysis and proposed amendments. Three outcomes are possible: Accept as proposed; Accept with modifications; Reject with a documented reason. All three outcomes are logged.

**Step 6. Amendment Integration**
Accepted amendments are integrated into the affected entry. The Amendment Record is updated. The entry's edition number is incremented.

**Step 7. Volume Edition Publication**
When one or more entries in a volume have been amended, a new edition of that volume is prepared and published. The new edition is distributed through all open access channels simultaneously.

**Step 8. Archive Preservation**
The previous edition is preserved permanently in the public archive before the new edition is released. Every edition of every volume remains publicly accessible indefinitely.

## VI. EDITORIAL AUTHORITY: THE HUMAN IN THE LOOP

The Claude API is the engine. Rickey Allan Howard is the author. These roles are distinct and the distinction is non-negotiable.

### What the AI Does

- Monitors legal developments continuously.
- Analyzes new developments against existing entries.
- Drafts proposed amendments.
- Prepares Amendment Record entries.
- Assigns priority flags.
- Never publishes anything without human approval.

### What the Author Does

- Reviews every proposed amendment before publication.
- Makes all final editorial decisions.
- Ensures every amendment is consistent with the Counter-Canon's foundational methodology.
- Ensures the Eternal Zone is never altered.
- Writes new entries for new headwords.
- Determines when a volume is ready for a new edition.
- Maintains the integrity of the Counter-Canon as a unified work of scholarship.

### The Editorial Standard

Every amendment published in the Counter-Canon must meet the same standard as the original entries: grounded in the law's own language, supported by specific legal authority, and consistent with the foundational principle that truth exists in the law's own text waiting to be found. No amendment may introduce unsupported assertion. No amendment may alter the Eternal Zone. No amendment may be published without the author's explicit approval.

**KEY POINT:** The AI handles the volume. The author maintains the integrity. This division is what allows the Counter-Canon to be both comprehensive and trustworthy. Comprehensiveness without integrity is noise. Integrity without comprehensiveness is irrelevance. The architecture achieves both.

## VIII. THE ENTRY FORMAT STANDARD: IMMUTABLE SPECIFICATION

Every entry in every volume of the Counter-Canon, in every edition from the first to the last, follows this exact format. This standard is immutable. It does not change between volumes. It does not change between editions. Consistency is what makes the Counter-Canon a unified work rather than a collection of individual essays.

**1. THE WORD** [Eternal Zone — never changes]
The headword. Part of speech. May include sub-entries for closely related legal terms.

**2. DOMINANT DEFINITION** [Living Zone — updates when authoritative sources revise]
The definition as Black's Law Dictionary, Merriam-Webster's Law Dictionary, or the dominant judicial usage states it. Quoted accurately with source citation. Never misrepresented.

**3. EMBEDDED ASSUMPTION** [Eternal Zone — never changes]
The unstated premise within the dominant definition. What does it assume about power? Who was it written for? What does it exclude? This is historical and analytical fact. It does not change.

**4. COUNTER-DEFINITION** [Living Zone — updates as law develops]
The alternate, legally grounded definition drawn from the law's own alternate uses of the word. Expanded and refined as new authority develops. Never invented — always found in the law.

**5. AUTHORITY** [Living Zone — updated with new citations]
The specific legal authorities supporting the counter-definition. Every citation in full. Updated when new decisions, statutes, or instruments provide additional support or when existing citations are superseded.

**6. PRACTICAL APPLICATION** [Living Zone — updated as procedure evolves]
How the counter-definition operates as a defense or claim in a real proceeding. Updated when procedural or substantive changes affect deployment.

**7. THE TRUTH** [Eternal Zone — never changes]
The irreducible statement. Not legal argument. The recognition that beneath all language and definition, something is simply and absolutely true. No court, no agency, no definition can ultimately contradict it.

## X. THE FOUNDING PRINCIPLES: WHAT THIS PROJECT WILL NEVER DO

The Counter-Canon is defined as much by what it will not do as by what it does. These principles are permanent. No edition, no amendment, and no future development of the project may violate them.

**1. The Counter-Canon will never misrepresent the dominant definition.**
Every dominant definition is quoted accurately from authoritative sources. The Counter-Canon contests definitions honestly — by engaging with what they actually say, not by distorting them into easier targets. Intellectual honesty is a founding principle.

**2. The Counter-Canon will never alter the Eternal Zone.**
The Embedded Assumption is a historical fact. The Truth is a spiritual and natural fact. Neither is subject to legal revision. No case, no statute, and no editorial decision may change them. They are the anchor.

**3. The Counter-Canon will never make unsupported legal assertions.**
Every counter-definition is grounded in the law's own language. Every authority citation is real, accurate, and relevant. Nothing in the Counter-Canon is invented. If the law does not support the counter-definition, the counter-definition is not published until the law does.

**4. The Counter-Canon will never advocate for the rejection of law.**
The Counter-Canon is a demand that the law honor its own highest stated principles. It operates within the law to hold the law accountable. It is not a rejection of legal authority. It is a demand for legal integrity.

**5. The Counter-Canon will never charge for access.**
The living beings who most need this work are those who have been most systematically excluded from economic opportunity. A paywall between them and the legal language being used against them serves the system. The Counter-Canon serves the living being. It is and will always be free.

**6. The Counter-Canon will never publish without editorial review.**
The AI analyzes. The author decides. No amendment, no matter how well-supported by the AI's analysis, is published without human editorial approval. The author's judgment is the final filter between the engine and the published word.

**7. The Counter-Canon will never stop.**
The law will continue to evolve. The language will continue to be contested. The living being will continue to need tools to navigate both. The Counter-Canon is a permanent project. It does not have an end date. It has an amendment record that grows until the truth the dominant definitions suppressed is fully visible in the law itself.
