## ANTI-EXPLOITATION CLAUSES (CRITICAL)

### 1. NO BUYOUT CLAUSE
**No licensee may acquire the underlying IP for any price.** The Trust will not sell, transfer, or assign ownership under any circumstances.

### 2. AUTOMATIC TERMINATION TRIGGERS
License automatically terminates if:
- Licensee attempts to patent derivative works without written consent
- Licensee reverse-engineers trade secrets (frequency hopping algorithm)
- Licensee uses technology for illegal purposes or human rights violations
- Licensee fails to meet minimum guarantee 2 years consecutively

### 3. TECHNOLOGY UPDATE RIGHTS
Trust retains right to:
- Update technology and require licensees to implement updates (security/safety)
- License new components to existing licensees at negotiated rates
- Withhold updates from licensees in breach of terms

### 4. AUDIT RIGHTS
Trust has unlimited audit rights:
- Examine licensee books/records with 30-day notice
- Verify unit sales and royalty calculations
- Inspect manufacturing facilities for compliance
- If audit reveals >5% under-reporting, licensee pays audit costs + penalties

### 5. MOST FAVORED NATION (MFN)
If Trust offers better terms to any licensee, all existing licensees get option to match those terms.

### 6. NON-COMPETE DURING TERM
Licensees cannot develop competing technology using Trust IP during license term + 2 years after termination.
