(5) a Guardian Mode automatic logic-kill and asset-lock triggered upon
detection of cognitive dissonance in the Grantor; and (6) ISO 20022 instant

AI Co-Trustee -- an artificial intelligence agent whose authority, obligations,
and operational boundaries are simultaneously governed by positive law
(irrevocable trust instrument) and cryptographic geometric invariants.
