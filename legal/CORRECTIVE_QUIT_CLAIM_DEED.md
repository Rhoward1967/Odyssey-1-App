## CORRECTION OF DEED

This Corrective Quit Claim Deed is executed on **________________, 2026**, by **CHRISTLA HOWARD** ("Grantor"), for the purpose of correcting a scrivener's error in the Quit Claim Deed recorded in **Book 5782, Page 262**, Clarke County, Georgia Records, dated **January 7, 2026**.

---

## RECITALS

**WHEREAS**, on January 7, 2026, Grantor executed and recorded a Quit Claim Deed conveying certain real property to the Howard & Jones Bloodline Ancestral Trust, recorded in Book 5782, Page 262, Clarke County Records; and

**WHEREAS**, said deed incorrectly identified the Grantee as "Howard, Rickey and Howard, Christla, Trustees of the Howard & Jones Bloodline Ancestral Trust"; and

**WHEREAS**, the correct Co-Trustees of the Howard & Jones Bloodline Ancestral Trust, as designated in the Trust Agreement executed in January 2026, are **Christla Howard** and **Teara Howard**; and

**WHEREAS**, Rickey Allan Howard is a Grantor of the Trust, NOT a Trustee, and was incorrectly listed in the original deed; and

**WHEREAS**, Teara Howard is a Co-Trustee of the Trust and was omitted from the original deed; and

**WHEREAS**, this Corrective Deed is executed to correct said scrivener's error and clarify the record.

---

## CORRECTION

**NOW, THEREFORE**, the Grantor hereby declares and affirms that:

1. The deed recorded in **Book 5782, Page 262**, Clarke County Records, is hereby **CORRECTED** as follows:

   **ORIGINAL (INCORRECT) GRANTEE:**  
   "Howard, Rickey and Howard, Christla, Trustees of the Howard & Jones Bloodline Ancestral Trust"

   **CORRECTED GRANTEE:**  
   "**Christla Howard and Teara Howard, Co-Trustees of the Howard & Jones Bloodline Ancestral Trust**"

2. This correction is made solely to accurately reflect the Co-Trustees as designated in the Howard & Jones Bloodline Ancestral Trust Agreement.

3. All other terms, conditions, and provisions of the deed recorded in Book 5782, Page 262 remain unchanged and in full force and effect.

4. This Corrective Deed relates back to and is effective as of the date of the original deed, **January 7, 2026**.
