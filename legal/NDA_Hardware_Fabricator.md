## 1. PURPOSE

The parties wish to explore a business relationship for the design, fabrication, and assembly of a custom computer chassis and hardware prototype (the "Project") for ODYSSEY-1's proprietary technology platform. In connection with this relationship, each party may disclose certain confidential and proprietary information to the other.

## 2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

**"Confidential Information"** means any and all information disclosed by either party to the other, whether orally, in writing, electronically, or visually, including but not limited to:

- All design specifications, CAD files, 3D models, and technical drawings
- ODYSSEY-1 branding, trademarks, logos, and visual identity

- Custom hardware specifications and component lists
- Software integration requirements and firmware
- LCD display content and user interface designs
- RGB lighting control systems and patterns

- Any information marked as "Confidential" or reasonably understood to be confidential

### For Fabricator (Disclosing):

- Manufacturing processes and techniques

- Any information marked as "Confidential" or reasonably understood to be confidential

## 3. OBLIGATIONS OF RECEIVING PARTY

The Receiving Party agrees to:

### 3.1 Non-Disclosure

- **NOT disclose** Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent
- **NOT use** Confidential Information for any purpose other than the Project
- Protect Confidential Information with the same degree of care used to protect its own confidential information (minimum: reasonable care)

### 3.2 Limited Access

- Limit access to Confidential Information to employees, contractors, or advisors who:
  - Have a legitimate need to know for the Project
  - Are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as restrictive as this Agreement

### 3.3 No Reverse Engineering

- **NOT reverse engineer, decompile, or disassemble** any prototypes, designs, or systems
- **NOT attempt to discover** trade secrets or proprietary methods through analysis

### 3.4 Security Measures

- Store all physical documents in locked, secure locations
- Encrypt all electronic files containing Confidential Information
- Use secure communication channels for transmission
- Implement access controls and audit trails

## 4. EXCLUSIONS FROM CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

Confidential Information does NOT include information that:

1. Was publicly known at the time of disclosure
2. Becomes publicly known through no breach of this Agreement
3. Was rightfully in Receiving Party's possession before disclosure
4. Is independently developed by Receiving Party without use of Confidential Information
5. Is rightfully received from a third party without breach of confidentiality obligations
6. Is required to be disclosed by law (with prior notice to Disclosing Party)

## 6. NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION

### 6.1 Non-Compete (Fabricator)

For a period of **TWO (2) YEARS** from the completion or termination of the Project, Fabricator agrees NOT to:

- Design, manufacture, or sell computer chassis or hardware with substantially similar aesthetic or design features to the ODYSSEY-1 prototype
- Use the "Galaxy" visualization concept or similar space-themed aesthetics for competing products
- Disclose ODYSSEY-1's design approach to competitors or other clients

**Exception:** Generic, non-custom computer cases sold in Fabricator's standard product line are permitted if they do not incorporate ODYSSEY-1's proprietary design elements.

### 6.2 Non-Solicitation

For a period of **ONE (1) YEAR** from the completion or termination of the Project, Fabricator agrees NOT to:

- Solicit, hire, or contract with any ODYSSEY-1 employees or contractors
- Interfere with ODYSSEY-1's business relationships
