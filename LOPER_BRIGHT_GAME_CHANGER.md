## What Changed Legally?

### Chevron Deference (1984-2024) ☠️

**Dead. Buried. Gone.**

**What it was:**
1. Court asks: Is statute ambiguous?
2. If yes: Does agency interpretation seem reasonable?
3. If yes: Defer to agency

**Result:** Agency interpretation = presumed correct

---

### Loper Bright Standard (2024-Present) ⚡

**Alive. Active. Deadly.**

**What it is:**
1. Court independently interprets statute
2. Agency interpretation gets no deference
3. Agency must prove interpretation matches statutory text
4. Burden on agency, not challenger

**Result:** Agency interpretation = must be proven correct
