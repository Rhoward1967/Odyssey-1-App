## POST-PRODUCTION EFFECTS

### Color Grading:

- **Cool tones** for scientific/cosmic sections (blue, purple)
- **Warm tones** for biological/organic sections (green, gold)
- **Golden highlights** for sacred geometry elements
- **High contrast** for dramatic moments

### Motion Graphics:

- Smooth, cinematic camera moves
- No jarring cuts
- Use geometric transitions (circle wipes, fractal dissolves)
- Subtle particle effects throughout

### Typography:

- **Font:** Clean sans-serif (Montserrat or Raleway)
- **Size:** Large enough for mobile viewing
- **Color:** White with slight glow, or gold for emphasis
- **Placement:** Golden Ratio positioning (not centered)

### Audio:

- **Music:** 432 Hz tuning throughout
- **Voice:** Clear, confident, not rushed
- **Sound Design:** Subtle resonance tones, crystalline chimes
- **Mix:** Voice slightly above music, clear but atmospheric
