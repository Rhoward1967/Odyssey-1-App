## STEP 1: RECORD YOUR VOICEOVER üéôÔ∏è

### Equipment Needed:

- **Microphone:**
  - Budget: USB mic ($50-100) - Blue Yeti, Audio-Technica AT2020
  - Phone with voice recorder app (free, decent quality)
- **Recording Software:**
  - Audacity (free, Windows/Mac)
  - Adobe Audition (professional)
  - Your phone's Voice Memo app

### Recording Tips:

1. **Environment:**
   - Quiet room (no AC, traffic noise)
   - Use closet with clothes (natural sound dampening)
   - Record at night for minimal background noise

2. **Technique:**
   - Speak clearly, not too fast
   - Pause 2-3 seconds between major sections
   - Record in one take if possible (maintain energy)
   - If you mess up, just pause, then start that sentence again

3. **Settings:**
   - Sample Rate: 48kHz
   - Bit Depth: 24-bit
   - Format: WAV or MP3 (320kbps)

4. **Read from the script:**
   - `Sacred_Geometry_DNA_Theory_Full_Script.md`
   - Print it out or use large font on screen
   - Mark breathing points with slashes: "universe / isn't random"

### Post-Processing (Simple):

1. Open in Audacity
2. Effect ‚Üí Noise Reduction (sample silent section first)
3. Effect ‚Üí Normalize (brings volume to -3dB)
4. Effect ‚Üí Compressor (evens out volume)
5. Export as MP3 (320kbps) or WAV

**Result: `Sacred_Geometry_Narration_FINAL.mp3`**

### Editing Workflow (DaVinci Resolve):

#### Step 1: Project Setup

1. Create New Project: "Sacred Geometry Final"
2. Set Timeline Resolution: 1920x1080, 60fps
3. Import all assets:
   - Your narration audio file
   - All generated images/videos
   - Stock footage
   - Screen recordings

#### Step 2: Audio First (Edit to Narration)

1. Drag narration to Timeline (Audio Track 1)
2. Listen through, mark sections with Timeline Markers:
   - M key = add marker
   - Name each marker (Scene 1A, Scene 2B, etc.)
3. This becomes your "map" for visuals

#### Step 3: Add Visuals

1. Match visuals to narration using your storyboard
2. Drag images/videos to Video Track 1 above narration
3. Timing guide:
   - Images: 3-5 seconds each
   - Animations: Full length (5-15 seconds)
   - Stock footage: As needed

#### Step 4: Transitions

1. Right-click between clips ‚Üí Add Transition
2. Use:
   - **Cross Dissolve** (smooth fades) - most common
   - **Circle Wipe** (for sacred geometry sections)
   - **Dip to Black** (between major sections)
3. Duration: 0.5-1 second

#### Step 5: Text Overlays

1. Effects Library ‚Üí Titles ‚Üí Text
2. Drag to Timeline above video
3. Edit text in Inspector panel:
   - Font: Montserrat or Raleway (Google Fonts)
   - Size: Large (readable on phone)
   - Color: White with 10% black outline
   - Position: Upper third or lower third

**Key Text Overlays from Storyboard:**

- Scene 1C: "Same Pattern. Different Scale."
- Scene 2B: Location names (Egypt, China, etc.)
- Scene 3A: "Schumann Resonance: 7.83 Hz"

#### Step 8: Color Grading

1. Switch to Color page
2. Apply LUT (Look-Up Table):
   - "Cinematic Cool" for space/cosmic scenes
   - "Warm Golden" for sacred geometry scenes
   - "Scientific Blue" for DNA/molecular scenes

3. Or manual adjustments:
   - Lift (shadows): Slightly blue for cosmic scenes
   - Gamma (midtones): Neutral or slightly warm
   - Gain (highlights): Slightly golden for sacred geometry
   - Saturation: +10 to +20 for more vibrant colors

1. Go to Deliver page
2. Settings:

3. Filename: `Sacred_Geometry_DNA_Theory_FINAL.mp4`
4. Render
