## LEGAL BASIS

This security interest is created pursuant to:

1. **Uniform Commercial Code (UCC) Article 9** - Secured Transactions
2. **Common Law Right of Self-Ownership** - Every living man/woman owns their own labor and body
3. **13th Amendment to the U.S. Constitution** - Prohibition of involuntary servitude (no one can claim ownership of another's labor without consent)
4. **Priority of Security Interests** - First-filed security interest has priority over later creditors
