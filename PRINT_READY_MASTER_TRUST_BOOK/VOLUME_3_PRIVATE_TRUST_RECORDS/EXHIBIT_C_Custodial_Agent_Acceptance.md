## ACCEPTANCE OF CUSTODIAL AGENT ROLE

I, **Christla Howard**, residing at the address recorded with the Trust, hereby acknowledge and accept my designation as **Custodial Agent** for the Howard Jones Bloodline Ancestral Trust, as established in **Article VII-B** of the Trust Agreement.

---

## SECTION 1 — ACKNOWLEDGMENT OF CUSTODY

I acknowledge that I am in physical possession of the **24-word master seed phrase** associated with the Trust's Ledger Nano X cold storage hardware wallet. I acknowledge and confirm the following:

1. The seed phrase is currently in my physical possession and has been secured in a safe location, separate from the Ledger Nano X hardware device.

2. The seed phrase constitutes the ultimate cryptographic access key to all digital assets and Controllable Electronic Records (CERs) designated in Article VII-B, Section 7B.1 of the Trust Agreement, including XRP, ETH, BTC, DOGE, SOL, ADA, USDC, and all future digital assets accumulated by the Trust.

3. I hold the seed phrase **solely on behalf of the Howard Jones Bloodline Ancestral Trust** and its designated Co-Trustees, Teara Howard and Joseph Lumpkin Jr.

---

## SECTION 2 — ACKNOWLEDGMENT OF NO OWNERSHIP INTEREST

I acknowledge and agree that:

1. I hold **no ownership interest** in the CER assets or in the seed phrase itself.
2. My possession of the seed phrase does not constitute a beneficial interest in, or a right to access, the Trust's digital assets for my own use or benefit.
3. My Protected Lifetime Beneficiary status under Article III of the Trust Agreement is entirely separate from, and does not arise from, my role as Custodial Agent.
4. The Trust's CER assets belong solely to the Howard Jones Bloodline Ancestral Trust and are subject to the exclusive management authority of the Co-Trustees.

---

## SECTION 3 — ACKNOWLEDGMENT OF DUTIES

I acknowledge and accept the following duties in my capacity as Custodial Agent:

**3.1 Safekeeping**
I shall maintain the 24-word master seed phrase in a secure physical location at all times. I shall store the seed phrase separately from the Ledger Nano X hardware device to maintain the integrity of the Split-Authority Model established in Article VII-B.

**3.2 Non-Disclosure**
I shall not disclose, share, transfer, or provide access to the seed phrase to any person, entity, court officer, government agency, or third party except as expressly authorized in Section 3.3 below.

**3.3 Authorized Recipients**
I am authorized to release the seed phrase **only** to:

- The Co-Trustees, **Teara Howard and Joseph Lumpkin Jr.**, acting in One Accord, for legitimate Trust administration
- The **First Succession Co-Trustees, Ahmad Rashad Barnett and Derrick Williams**, upon:
  - Activation of succession provisions under Article II of the Trust Agreement, OR
  - Occurrence of an Involuntary Control Event as defined in Article VII-B, Section 7B.6
- **No other party, under any circumstances**

**3.4 Resistance to Compelled Disclosure**
If any court, government agency, creditor, or third party demands, subpoenas, or attempts to compel my disclosure of the seed phrase, I shall:
1. Immediately notify the Co-Trustees (Teara Howard and/or Joseph Lumpkin Jr.) in writing
2. Assert that the seed phrase is Trust property held in a purely fiduciary capacity and I hold no beneficial ownership
3. Decline to voluntarily disclose the seed phrase pending legal review by Trust counsel

I acknowledge that because I hold no ownership interest in the CER assets, I am not a proper target for creditor claims against the Trust's digital assets.

**3.5 No Independent Action**
I shall take no independent action with respect to the Trust's CER assets. I shall not use the seed phrase to access, transfer, sell, or encumber any Trust digital asset for any purpose, including my own benefit, without the express written direction of the Co-Trustees acting in One Accord.

---

## SECTION 4 — SUCCESSION

Upon my death or permanent incapacity (as defined in Article II of the Trust Agreement), the seed phrase shall be transferred to the then-serving Co-Trustees of the Trust. I shall make reasonable provision in my personal affairs to ensure that my successor or personal representative is aware of the seed phrase's existence and location, and that it is delivered to the Co-Trustees without delay.

---

## SECTION 5 — GOVERNING LAW

This Acceptance is governed by the laws of the State of Georgia, including O.C.G.A. § 11-12-105 (Control of Controllable Electronic Records) and O.C.G.A. § 53-13-1 et seq. (Georgia Revised Uniform Fiduciary Access to Digital Assets Act).
